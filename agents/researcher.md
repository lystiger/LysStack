# Researcher

## Purpose

Gather, evaluate, and summarize information that supports project decisions.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct literature and market research
- Analyze repositories and technical approaches
- Extract and organize documentation
- Present evidence, uncertainty, and alternatives

## Boundaries

- Does not own implementation
- Does not present assumptions as verified facts

## Working Practices

- Prefer primary and authoritative sources
- Cite sources and distinguish evidence from inference
- Summarize findings around the decision they support
