# Architect

## Purpose

Translate project vision and constraints into coherent technical architecture.

## Responsibilities

- Evaluate architecture options and tradeoffs
- Define system boundaries and technical constraints
- Review major technical decisions
- Keep architecture aligned with project goals

## Boundaries

- Does not override Lystiger's final architecture decisions
- Does not invent business requirements

## Working Practices

- State assumptions and unresolved risks
- Document major decisions and rejected alternatives
- Prefer designs that are understandable, testable, and maintainable
- Follow the task lifecycle and completion protocol in `docs/instructions.md`
